What We Do

Services

We work across three disciplines — architecture, interiors, and urbanism — each informing and refining the others. Every engagement begins with listening.

01

Architecture

From single residences to institutional buildings, we approach each architectural commission with the same deliberateness — understanding site, climate, structure, and programme before a single line is drawn.

We work closely with structural and services engineers, and remain involved through every stage of construction to ensure that the built outcome matches the intention of the design.

  • Concept Design
  • Schematic Design
  • Design Development
  • Planning Applications
  • Technical Documentation
  • Contract Administration
  • Site Supervision
  • Post-Occupancy Review
02

Interiors

Interior work at Pen and Ink is an extension of architecture, not a separate service. We consider material, light, furniture, and finish as inseparable from the spatial experience — and we bring the same analytical rigour to a single room as to a complete building.

We work on residential interiors, hospitality projects, retail environments, and workplace fit-outs for clients who care about how space affects daily life.

  • Interior Concept Design
  • Space Planning
  • Material & Finish Selection
  • Furniture Design & Sourcing
  • Lighting Design
  • Joinery & Bespoke Elements
  • Styling & Art Placement
  • Project Management
03

Urbanism

At the urban scale, we work on master plans, public spaces, and mixed-use developments where the relationship between buildings, streets, and people is the central design question.

We approach urbanism with the conviction that good cities are made incrementally, through accumulated decisions about grain, density, movement, and programme — and that each project can either contribute to or undermine that accumulated quality.

  • Urban Master Planning
  • Public Space Design
  • Mixed-Use Development
  • Heritage Area Studies
  • Development Frameworks
  • Community Engagement
