Interior Design Studio — Website Concepts

Six minimal directions for Vishkar Interiors.

Six genuinely different layout structures — not one design in six colours. They differ in where the navigation sits (top-centre, a left rail, right-aligned), which way the page scrolls (down, sideways, full-screen snap), and how content is positioned (centred, two-pane, broken grid). Each also has its own loading animation. Shared throughout: black-and-white, Apple-minimal, sleek dropdown nav across all six menus, and booking a consultation as the primary action. Open any card to explore it, then tell us which to take forward.
